Annual Report design Sunnyside Motel

For this project, I was tasked with making an annual report for the company I came up with, Sunnyside Motel. Sunnyside Motel Is a luxury boutique hotel that has a 70s retro aesthetic: perfect for Instagram pictures or for anyone wanting a beautifully and uniquely decorated hotel room. It was difficult researching for this project, especially for accurate graphs and statistics to incorporate into the report. I also found it difficult to keep a consistent theme throughout the report. I decided to go with a heavily decorated report because it would match the brand identity of Sunnyside Motel and would make it a bit more bearable to read all the way through.
